About Me

I’m Ana and I’m a junior multimedia designer based on Oporto. I am highly motivated and multi-skilled with keen interest in design, photography and digital marketing.
I have embraced multiple projects while I was studying, like Coop AR, Downtown 80 and Anamnese.
For my dissertation I was able to build a project called StorySD. The goal is to teach entrepreuneurs how to apply transmedia storytelling to their communication strategy.


I'm currently working at Zappy Software as a multimedia designer and marketing manager.

In my free time I enjoy participating in game jams, hackathons, workshops and other events. I like to produce illustrations and improving my photography skills.
