About me

I’m Ana and I’m a junior multimedia designer based on Oporto. I am highly motivated and multi-skilled with keen interest in design, photography and digital marketing.
I hold a bachelor's in Plastic Arts - Multimedia from FBAUP and a master degree in Multimedia - Technologies from FEUP.

    • Coop AR , 2017

      • This project was developed for a thesis at the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto. CooP AR is a system that enables asynchronous collaboration between two users (e.g. between a mentor and an apprentice).

    • Future Places Citizen Lab: Behind the Curtain , 2016

      • This citizen lab introduces to it’s participants the “Hero’s Journey”, aka, monomyth. This concept was first introduced by Joseph Campbell in 1949 and it’s still used in these days to create successful stories for movies, books, etc. With the support of a camera, the audience performed and recorded a story made by themselves regarding the previous concept. The result of this experience is a stopmotion video.

    • Downtown 80 , 2016

      • This project was born at FEUP as a result of an assignment for the ”Multimedia Laboratory”, subject of the Master in Multimedia. Inspired by the book “Around the World in Eighty Days”, by Jules Verne, a new story has been made, using some of the original elements, just like the characters and the time. With this transmedia experience, users will interact both physically and virtually, while exploring Porto’s city centre and its local businesses

    • From the Present to the Future , 2013

      • The subway is an essential transport in big cities. Their stations, true works of architecture, are the starting point for some images that brings us to a fantastic underground world.
